What We Seek

We welcome partnerships and collaborations with international NGOs, research institutions, and funding organizations that share our commitment to improving public health.

By working together, we aim to build sustainable, evidence-driven solutions that strengthen health systems and expand opportunities for women, girls, and communities.

  • Design and implement maternal and reproductive health programs
  • Conduct joint, multi-country research and evaluation studies
  • Strengthen health systems and promote youth engagement in public health initiatives
