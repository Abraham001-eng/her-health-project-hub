Why the Her Health Project Hub is Unique

The Her Health Project Hub stands out in public health and evidence-based policy for its unique integration of research, service delivery, and advocacy. Unlike many initiatives focused solely on program implementation, the Hub ensures that every intervention and recommendation is firmly grounded in scientific evidence through the work of its dedicated team of researchers and technical experts.

At the center of this excellence is the Executive Director, whose diverse research experience spans continents, disciplines, and health systems. This breadth of work reflects a deep commitment to linking high-quality evidence with real-world impact—ensuring that research does not remain in academic spaces alone, but actively informs programs, policies, and community-level change.
