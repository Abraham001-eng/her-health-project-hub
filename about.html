About Her Health Project Hub

Who We Are

Her Health Project Hub is a women-focused nonprofit organization dedicated to transforming the health, well-being, and everyday lives of girls and women across all life stages. We exist to close the gaps in access to correct health information, quality care, and empowerment resources — especially for women in underserved communities.

Born out of a deep passion for women’s health equity, Her Health Project Hub serves as a central platform where women can learn, grow, get support, and take charge of their bodies with confidence.

Our Origin

Her Health Project Hub began with a simple but powerful observation: too many women were suffering in silence, lacking accurate information and safe spaces to ask questions about their health. From menstrual health to maternal well-being, from mental health to hygiene, the misinformation and stigma were overwhelming.

In January 2024, our founder Dr Hadiza Magaji Mahmoud — a passionate women’s health advocate — decided to take action. She launched Her Health Project Hub as a digital-first initiative designed to bridge the information gap and bring health education directly to women where they are: online, mobile, and within their communities.

What began as a small project quickly grew into a recognized movement, touching thousands of women through education, outreach, and empowerment programs.

Our Mission

To educate, empower, and support women by providing accessible, accurate, and culturally relevant health information — enabling them to make informed decisions and take control of their well-being.

Our Vision

A world where every woman, regardless of background or location, has the knowledge, support, and confidence needed to live a healthy, fulfilled, and empowered life.

    Meet Our Founder

    Dr Hadiza Magaji Mahmoud — Founder & Lead Advocate

    Her Health Project Hub was founded by a dedicated women’s health educator with years of experience in community outreach, wellness advocacy, and digital health communication.
