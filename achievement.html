Her Health Project: Stay Informed, Stay Safe

To wrap up 2024, we are excited to reflect on the success of our recent community outreach program at Kabuga Health Care, Kano State, aimed at improving birth preparedness and supporting expecting mothers and their families. This impactful event marked a meaningful step toward strengthening community health outcomes.

During this event, we:

  • Distributed over 50 birth kits, providing essential tools to ensure safe deliveries for expecting mothers.
  • Provided transportation support to ensure no one was left behind due to barriers to access.
  • Delivered comprehensive educational talks, including a health awareness session on Antenatal Care (ANC), presented by an experienced matron from Aminu Kano Teaching Hospital’s Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology, highlighting the importance of regular ANC visits.
  • Hosted a session on Emergency Awareness and Birth Preparedness, empowering women and families with knowledge to respond effectively during pregnancy, labor, and delivery.

At Her Health Project, we believe no woman should face the journey of motherhood without the right support and knowledge. By working closely with communities, we remain committed to improving maternal health and ensuring better outcomes for families.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to all participants, volunteers, and the dedicated team at Kabuga Health Care for making this outreach a success.

As we look ahead to 2025, we remain more committed than ever to advancing maternal health and building healthier, stronger communities. Thank you for your continued support as we work toward a world where her health truly matters.
