Resources – Her Health Project Hub

At Her Health Project Hub, we believe that access to clear, reliable and culturally relevant information is the first step toward empowering every woman to take control of her health. This Resource Page is designed to serve as a trusted library—carefully curated to support women, girls, caregivers, and communities with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions.

Below, you’ll find educational guides, downloadable materials, and practical tools organized to help women at every stage of life.

1. Women’s Health Guides

Easy-to-read resources written in simple, friendly language:

  • Menstrual Health Guide
    Understanding your cycle, PMS, hygiene, and myths vs facts.
  • Reproductive Health 101
    Fertility, pregnancy preparation, sexual health essentials.
  • Safe Motherhood Guide
    Antenatal care, danger signs, nutrition, emotional health, and postpartum care.
  • Breast Health Education
    How to perform a self-examination, early detection, and risk factors.
  • Mental & Emotional Wellness Toolkit
    Confidence-building, stress management, emotional stability, and self-care.
