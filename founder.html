Executive Director – Her Health Project Hub

Dr. Hadiza Magaji Mahmoud is a medical doctor and public health practitioner whose career began on the front lines of healthcare delivery in Northern Nigeria.

She has served women, children, and families across Kano, Katsina, and Jigawa States, working in Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Paediatrics, and General Medicine.

From busy delivery wards to neonatal units and complex medical cases, she witnessed both the resilience of patients and the systemic gaps within Nigeria’s healthcare system—experiences that shaped her deep commitment to equity and meaningful health reform.

Dr. Hadiza earned her MBBS and a Master’s degree in Reproductive Health from Bayero University, Kano, and later obtained an MSc in Public Health from the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine, University of London.

Her work spans clinical practice, public health research, and global health engagement. She has participated in international conferences across Dubai, Europe, and the United States.

These lived realities inspired the creation of Her Health Project Hub—an integrated platform dedicated to advocacy, research, and empowerment for women’s health in Nigeria.

Today, she leads with compassion, clarity, and evidence-driven expertise, bridging research and action, local realities and global best practices.

Her leadership reflects a steadfast belief: when women’s health is prioritized, entire communities thrive.
