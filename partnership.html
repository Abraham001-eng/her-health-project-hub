Why We Seek Partnership

Her Health Project Hub seeks strategic partnerships and grant support from organizations such as the World Health Organization (WHO), Sasakawa Africa Association (SAA), Clinton Foundation, and other global development partners to scale up our work in:

✔ Maternal and reproductive health improvement
✔ Health systems strengthening
✔ Research and data capacity building
✔ Youth and women’s empowerment
✔ Community resilience and innovation

We believe that by combining global insights with local expertise, we can co-create solutions that deliver measurable, equitable, and lasting health outcomes for women, families, and communities in Nigeria and across sub-Saharan Africa.
